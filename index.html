Adverto

A private publishing tool for our own faceless video channels.

Adverto is a desktop command-line tool run by its owner on a personal computer. It writes scripts, renders short vertical videos from generated frames and stock footage, and uploads the finished files to the social accounts that the owner controls — currently YouTube and TikTok.

Who uses it

Only the owner of the accounts it posts to. Adverto is not a service, has no sign-up, no users other than its owner, and is not offered to anyone else.

How TikTok is used

  • Login Kit — the owner authorizes their own TikTok account once, in a browser.
  • Content Posting API — the tool uploads a rendered video to that same account, with the caption and privacy level chosen before the upload.
